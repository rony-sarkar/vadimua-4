Seat Belt Repair After Accident

After a vehicle has been in an accident, the seat belt retractors and/or buckle pre-tensioners may deploy to protect the passengers and minimize any injury. At this point, the seat belts must be replaced or rebuilt in order to properly function again.

Our seat belt rebuild service uses OEM-grade components to safely repair the mechanism for continual operation. We repair each seat belt to function like new, both in everyday operation and any potential accident deployment. Everything is tested thoroughly prior to shipping your unit back to you.

Depending on your particular vehicle, the seat belt mechanism may be equipped with a more advanced deployment system. These can be categorized into single-stage, dual-stage, and triple-stage seat belts.
